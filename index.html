Perigrene Attack!

Birds. You see them everyday on the street. But do you really see them?

You are the Peregrine Falcon. Drawn to the scaling heights of the world's largest cities, you mericilessly stalk your prey: the unsuspecting, aimless pigeon.

But wait, buster. You are not alone here. In this urban landscape, you must daily contend with your sworn enemy: the flying pig. As you sail the sky, avoid the avian swine. They could be the death of you, literally.

Use the arrow keys to guide your winged avatar through the urban firmament. Every pigeon you eat scores you a point. Every pig you hit makes you lose two.

Two players?
Player Two, you use the keys a, w, s, d.

Advance to the next level by beating the 'pigeons to eat' threshold. As you progress, your score will reset and the threshold will increase. Luckily, the number of pigeons will too. And so will the pigs...

Congratulations! Onto the next level!
